Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-20

Overview

PDS MOOver performs migrations in your browser. Where possible, operations are client side to minimize collection of personal data by the server. Backups happen on our servers, and your data is stored in a cloud base object store we have access to

Information We May Receive

  • If you use optional features (e.g., backups), we take a backup of your AT Proto repo when it has a change, any blobs you have posted to your account are stored in a object store that we control. Just like your AT Proto data this is public and accessible to anyone. But this does not mean anyone is able to take this data and impersonate you without what is known as a Rotation Key.

What We Do Not Do

  • If you use PDS MOOver just for migrations, missing blobs, or turn off feature. We do not collect or store any information. This happens all client side.
  • We do not collect any personal information beyond your current PDS host and your DID if you sign up for backups.
  • Your passwords are only ever sent to your PDS. Your passwords are never collected, and all authenticated requests happen proxied via your PDS to our services for backups

Retention

Server-side logs may be retained for a limited time for security, debugging, and rate-limiting. This is short term logging and cleared on instance reboots and kept no longer than 60 days. Backup-related data, if enabled by you, is stored indefinitely for the life of this service unless you opt out via backups and click delete to remove all of your backups and from any future ones.

Your Choices

You may choose not to use the service. If you proceed, ensure you have backups and understand the migration risks. If you ever want to be removed from our services you can also login via backups and click delete to remove all of your backups and from any future ones.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Service Provider and Location

All backup data is currently being stored on UpCloud's object store in a data center located in the US. UpCloud is also our service provider for the VPS running the services. We may change service providers in the future, if we do this terms of service will be updated to reflect that.
